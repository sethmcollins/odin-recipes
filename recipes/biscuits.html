Easy Cheese Biscuits

Description

These biscuits are easy to make and packed with flavor from the cheese. You can also add garlic for some additional oomph! Your taste buds will thank you.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/2 cup cold butter
  • 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 garlic clove minced
  • 1-1/4 cups milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450Â°.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, sugar, salt and cream of tartar.
  3. Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. You can do this with a food processor or with two forks in a criss-cross motion.
  4. Stir in cheese and garlic.
  5. Add milk; stir just until moistened.
  6. Drop dough by heaping 1/4 cupfuls 2 inches apart onto a greased baking sheet.
  7. Bake 10-15 minutes or until golden brown.
