Easy Pancakes

Description

These biscuits are easy to make and packed with flavor from the cheese. You can also add garlic for some additional oomph! Your taste buds will thank you.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.
  2. Make a well in the center, and pour in milk, egg and oil. Mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  5. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
